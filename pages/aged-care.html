What You Need

Essential qualifications and skills for aged care careers

Qualifications

  • Certificate III in Individual Support (Ageing) - We can help you obtain this
  • First Aid and CPR certification
  • Working with Vulnerable People check
  • English proficiency (IELTS 7.0 or equivalent)

Personal Qualities

  • Compassionate and patient nature
  • Strong communication skills
  • Physical fitness for hands-on care
  • Cultural sensitivity and respect

Work Conditions

  • Flexible shift patterns available
  • Weekend and public holiday work
  • Physical demands of care work
  • Emotional resilience required
